About Us

Meet the founders: Beth and Maddie

We created SFT because we love to travel but felt as though it was hard to find accurate information about how safe women feel going solo in different parts of the world.

We hope that this website will enable more women to feel empowered about travelling the world alone.

Maddie comes from Reading and Beth comes from the New Forest. We met during a graduate scheme at Vodafone in London and bonded over a shared love for travelling the world and eating all the food possible.

Any questions, let us know!

What is the most Instagram worthy spot you've ever been?

Beth: Pigeon Point, Tobago. It is on the front of guide books for a reason!

Maddie: Angkor Wat, Cambodia. One of the few places I've been that lives up to the hype.

Where has been your favourite place you've travelled to?

Beth: Vietnam! The people are so friendly and the food is amazing.

Maddie: South Africa. Volunteering there was the happiest I've ever been.

What has been the best experience you've ever had?

Beth: It's a toss up between safari in South Africa's Kruger National Park and snorkelling with turtles off the coast of Gili T!

Maddie: Night diving in Koh Samui, earning my advanced PADI qualification.

Quick fire questions!

Your most asked queries about Beth and Maddie
What is the best dish you've ever had and where?

Beth: Carbonara in Rome. Nothing beats a carb-fest.

Maddie: Banh Xeo in Vietnam. I love streetfood.

Where is one place that you would never go back to?

Beth: Koh Phangan. I went not on the full moon party week and it was dead!

Maddie: Lapland. I spent the whole time crying because I was so cold!

What is your number one top travel tip?

Beth: If there is something that you know you want to do, make sure you book tickets in advance! Skip the queue tickets are 100% worth it if you're on a tight schedule.

Maddie: Wherever you're going, make sure you know how to say hello, thank you and please.
